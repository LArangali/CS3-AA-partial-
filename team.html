Haikyuu!!

Daichi Sawamura (#1) - Captain/Wing Spiker/Opposite Hitter (3rd Year): Daichi is the team captain and plays as a wing spiker and opposite hitter. He is known for his exceptional receiving and leadership skills. Daichi is a dependable player both on and off the court, often keeping the team together during matches.



Kōshi Sugawara (#2) - Vice-Captain/Setter/Pinch Server (3rd Year): Sugawara serves as the vice-captain and plays as the team's setter. He is a strategic and skilled player, often setting up plays for his teammates. He is also known for his ability to serve as a pinch server when needed.


Asahi Azumane (#3) - Wing Spiker/Outside Hitter/Ace (3rd Year): Asahi is a powerful wing spiker and outside hitter, known as the team's ace. He possesses strong spiking abilities and is often relied upon to score crucial points for Karasuno.


Yū Nishinoya (#4) - Libero (2nd Year): Nishinoya is the team's libero, specializing in defensive play. He is incredibly agile, known for his spectacular diving saves, and often considered the guardian of the team's backcourt.


Ryunosuke Tanaka (#5) - Wing Spiker/Outside Hitter (2nd Year): Tanaka is a wing spiker and outside hitter, providing valuable attacking options for the team. He is known for his aggressive and energetic playing style, often acting as a motivator for the team.


Chikara Ennoshita (#6) - Wing Spiker/Outside Hitter (2nd Year): Ennoshita is another wing spiker and outside hitter on the team. He is a reliable player and often takes the court when needed, providing stability to the team's lineup.


Hisashi Kinoshita (#7) - Wing Spiker/Outside Hitter (2nd Year): Kinoshita is yet another wing spiker and outside hitter for Karasuno. While not as prominent as some other players, he contributes to the team's overall depth and versatility.


Kazuhito Narita (#8) - Middle Blocker (2nd Year): Narita plays as a middle blocker, specializing in blocking and net defense. He works in tandem with the other middle blockers to disrupt the opponent's attacks.


Tobio Kageyama (#9) - Setter (1st Year): Kageyama is a highly skilled setter, known for his precision and technique. Despite being a first-year student, he quickly becomes a key player for Karasuno, forming a strong connection with the team's spikers.


Shōyō Hinata (#10) - Middle Blocker (1st Year): Hinata starts as a middle blocker but later transitions into a wing spiker. He is known for his incredible speed, jumping ability, and determination. He's often referred to as the "Little Giant" due to his exceptional agility. And in the Manga he is known as "Ninja Shoyo"


Kei Tsukishima (#11) - Middle Blocker (1st Year): Tsukishima is a first-year middle blocker, known for his height and blocking skills. He provides a formidable presence at the net, making it challenging for opponents to score.


Tadashi Yamaguchi (#12) - Pinch Server (1st Year): Yamaguchi is a pinch server who comes off the bench to serve at crucial moments. He is working on improving his serving skills and becoming more reliable in high-pressure situations.
